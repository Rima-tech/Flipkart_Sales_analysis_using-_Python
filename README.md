# Flipkart_Sales_analysis_using-_Python
Exploratory Data Analysis on the sale of Flipkart products.

### Project Overview 
 This data analysis aims to provide insights into the sales performance of flipkart over a past few months .
 
### Techniques Used
Data Analysis & Data Visualization

### Built with
Jupyter Notebook

### DataSet
link to data set:-https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/PromptCloudHQ/flipkart-products


### Objective
1.What are the top performing products and brands?

2.Which brands are offering the highest discount?

3.What are some of the target customers that spend the most money on flipkart?

4.A thorough analysis of product ratings.

5.What is the proportion of 5 star rated products to the total products?


6.What are the best and worst performing products and brands in terms of ratings?

7.What are the best and worst performing Months?

8.Is there any trend associated with  months and discount price ?

9.When are customers the most active during the day?



### Libraries Used
Numpy

Pandas

Matplotlib

Plotly

### conclusion 
1.The top product customers are purchasing is 'Clothing', whereas, the top brand is 'Allure Auto'.

2.Brands giving highest discounts are 'Rajcrafts', 'Bling', 'Instella' and more. 

3.Found out the unique Ids of top customers .

4.Out of 20,000 products only 620 products have 5 star ratings. 

5.The top performing Brand is 'Regular' and The top product category is 'Clothing ' in terms of ratings.

6.During December 2015 & January 2016 products sales observed as high in numbers while February had the lowest.

7.In the month of April Avarage discount(37%) was relatively little higher then other months, And the avarage discount fluctuating within a narrow range of 37% to 44%.

8.AT 6 am, 12 am and 12 pm most customer activity have been noticed.
